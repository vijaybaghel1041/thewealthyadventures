The Coffee Table Invest

It all started over a cup of chai on a Sunday afternoon.

Arjun, Meera, and Vikram had been friends since college—bound not just by memories, but by a common worry in their late 20s: "Are we saving enough?" “Are we investing right?” “Where does our money even go every month?”

One day, instead of gossiping about office life or Netflix shows, they decided to do something radical. They opened a spreadsheet. Together.

They called it The Coffee Table Portfolio.

Each of them contributed ₹2,000 per month—less than a night out. They met every month at a coffee shop, reviewed stocks, SIPs, mutual funds, and brainstormed startups they admired. Over time, they learned about compounding, took small risks, lost a bit, gained more—and grew smarter with every decision.

Five years later, the Coffee Table Portfolio had grown into a solid investment fund. But more importantly, it had grown their confidence. They now had shared assets, a startup idea in motion, and something no bank could offer: a tribe of trust.

“You don’t need to be rich to invest. You just need the right friends.”

What started as friendship turned into financial freedom—powered not by apps, but by accountability and curiosity.
